## 2. Competitor SEO Analysis

### 2.1 PragueScort.cz (strongest EN competitor)

**Title:** `Escort Prague - Verified Girls, Real Photos & Direct Booking | Praguescort`
**Meta description:** Not found in HTML (relies on Google snippet generation)
**H1:** Not accessible (heavy JS/CSS rendering)
**JSON-LD:** None detected
**Hreflang:** None (single-language site)
**OG tags:** Not accessible (CSS/JS-heavy page)

**SEO strengths:**
- Strong blog/content strategy (guides, FAQ, reviews)
- URL structure: `/service/elite-escort/`, `/faq/`, `/prague-escort-reviews-...`
- Targets long-tail keywords via blog posts: "how to find best escort agencies Prague"
- Categories: standard, premium, elite, GFE, travel companion
- FAQ page with common questions

**SEO weaknesses:**
- No structured data (JSON-LD)
- No hreflang (English only)
- No meta descriptions on many pages
- Heavy WordPress/JS — SSR unclear

**Keywords targeted:**
- "escort Prague", "verified girls Prague", "real photos escort", "direct booking"
- "elite escort Prague", "GFE Prague", "outcall escorts Prague"
- "best escort agencies Prague"

**Title:** `Home | Escort Prague 24h service in Prague and all Czechia`

## 5. Title/Description Patterns from Competitors

### Common title patterns:
- `{Service} {City} - {USP} | {Brand}` (PragueScort)
- `{Brand} -- {Czech description with keywords}` (PrivatPrague)
- `{Page} | {Brand} {City} {Differentiator}` (EP24h)

### Common meta description patterns:
- First person narrative (GentleMeet): "I am a luxurious companion..."
- Feature list: "Verified profiles, real photos, direct booking"
- Stats/numbers: "13 verified companions, 4 apartments"

### Best practices observed:
- Include city name (Praha/Prague/Prag) in every title
- Include differentiator (verified, luxury, 24h, discreet)
- Numbers work well (specific counts of companions, locations)
- Action words: "booking", "contact", "browse"
